Core Formulas

Base‑i Encoding Formula:

E[n] = x[n] · i^(n mod 4)

Inverse Reconstruction:

x[n] = Re( E[n] · i^(-n mod 4) )

Where:

  • x[n] is the original signal value at time/sample n
  • i is the imaginary unit
  • mod 4 cycles through: 1, i, -1, -i
