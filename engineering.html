Introduction

Traditional FFT methods introduce residual imaginary components that limit reconstruction fidelity. Our novel Base-i and Mod-N encoding methods achieve zero-loss reconstruction with unprecedented precision.

Performance Highlights

Mod-12 and Mod-24 encoding methods outperform FFT across every tested waveform, with perfect reconstruction (MSE = 0, Abs. Err. = 0) and zero residual imaginary signal. FFT, in contrast, introduces distortions up to 21.77 in residual imaginary amplitude.
